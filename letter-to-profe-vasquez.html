Letter to Profe. Vasquez

Letter correspondence with Profe. Vasquez, Performance Studies professor at Yale.

By Camila Tiburcio Rubio
January 12, 2024 • 4 min read
YaleLetter

I hope you’re well. I recently discovered that the second reader for my master thesis at CLACS does not have to be an NYU faculty member, and I was immediately excited at the prospect of you being my second reader if you were willing! I believe that when it comes time to conduct the thesis committee, we could do it on zoom as well.

Also, I was wondering if you would be interested in writing me a letter of recommendation for NYU performance studies and Yale American studies PhD’s, among other programs. You are obviously very well respected in both these circles, and the field of performance studies in general, a letter from you would be very meaningful and what I would hope to be the start of our partnership moving forward.

I would be more than happy to provide you with some samples of my work and answer any questions you may have so that you could familiarize yourself with myself and my writing before you write, or choose, to write the letter. I completely understand If you do not feel comfortable doing either Thank you in advance!
